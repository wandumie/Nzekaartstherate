BACKGROUND

Established in 2020, Nzeka Arts was introduced to cultivate, nurture and expose various talents from young people through drama, poetry, music, dances and other creative activities.
Nzeka Arts is aimed at supporting the talented youths in making good use of their talent and help them benefit from it.Furthermore, Nzeka Arts gives opportunity to the youths who have passion for arts to express themselves. The members are encouraged, supported and challenged as they try to maximize their potential in performance .

OBJECTVES

We aim at accomplishing a number of goals.


  • To provide a platform that will help the youths realize and utilize their inborn talents, thereby becoming prominent in society.

  • To help the youths acknowledge various cultures from different ethnic groups or races to appreciate cultural diversity and adapt positively.

  • To help the youths understand different situations in life and acquire essential life skills that will help them respond positively.

  • To educate, inform, and sensitize people on various social issues affecting their lives.

  • To help students have a clear understanding of some concepts in the academic circles.

  • To entertain people and bring the spirit of togetherness regardless of individual differences.

  • To act as an instrument of change for behavior and refrain young people from indulging in immorality.

  • To help in bridging the generational gap and adjusting to the technological advancement of the world.

OUR MISSION

Empowering the youth by fostering the realization and utilization of their innate talents, promoting cultural appreciation, and equipping them with essential life skills. We aim to educate, inform, and engage individuals on critical social issues, support academic growth, and cultivate a spirit of unity and positive behavior. By bridging generational gaps and embracing technological advancements, we strive to shape a responsible and inclusive society
